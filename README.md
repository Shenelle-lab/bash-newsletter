# Bash Staff News — Crew Newsletter 2026

The official crew newsletter for the **Mundi Mundi Bash** 2026 season, published by the OMFG organising team. A single-page static HTML newsletter deployed on Vercel.

🌐 **Live site:** [bash-newsletter.vercel.app](https://bash-newsletter.vercel.app)

---

## What's inside

- Welcome message from the team
- Big Red Bash 2026 cancellation update
- What's new at Mundi 2026 — South Plaza Arts Hub, new shade domes, NRMA sponsorship, Lightfest
- Meet the Team — Brookvale & Blackheath offices
- Farewell to Maggie McKay
- Key dates timeline — 9–25 August 2026
- Staying in touch — Facebook crew group, email, WhatsApp
- NSW Tourism & Events Awards 2025 — Gold winner
- Safety tips & site info
- Country, community & culture — Wilyakali Country, Belmont Station, Silverton

---

## Tech

- Pure HTML/CSS — no build step, no dependencies
- Fonts via Google Fonts (Montserrat)
- All assets self-contained (inline)
- Static site hosted on [Vercel](https://vercel.com)

## Updating

Push to `main` and Vercel redeploys automatically within ~30 seconds.
